Travel Tips for Mumbai

1. Stay Hydrated

Mumbai can get quite hot and humid, especially during the summer months. Be sure to drink plenty of water and stay hydrated.

2. Use Public Transport

Public transport in Mumbai is efficient and cost-effective. Consider using local trains, buses, or taxis to get around.

3. Be Mindful of Your Belongings

Keep an eye on your belongings and avoid carrying too much cash. Mumbai is generally safe, but it's always good to be cautious.
