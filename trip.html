Travel Tips

Make your trip to Mumbai smooth and enjoyable with these essential tips:

  • Carry cash as some local vendors may not accept credit cards.
  • Use reliable transportation services like Uber or Ola.
  • Stay hydrated and carry a refillable water bottle, especially when exploring outdoor attractions.
  • Dress modestly when visiting religious sites to respect local customs.
  • Be vigilant of your belongings, particularly in crowded areas.
  • Learn basic local phrases or use a translation app to enhance communication.
